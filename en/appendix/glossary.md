# Glossary of Terms

> Core terminology used throughout this chronicle, organized by category. Each entry includes the Warhammer term, the Chinese translation used in this project, and a brief explanation.

---

## Warhammer 40,000 — Core Terms

### Factions & Organizations

| English | 中文 | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Imperium of Man | 人类帝国 | The largest human polity in the galaxy — totalitarian, theocratic, militarized |
| Chaos | 混沌 | The destructive forces of the Warp and their mortal followers |
| Eldar / Aeldari | 灵族 | Ancient, once-dominant race; now shattered into multiple surviving factions |
| Orks | 兽人 | Warlike green-skinned xenos; reproduce via fungal spores |
| Necrons | 太空死灵 | Ancient mechanical undead; once ruled the galaxy |
| Tyranids | 泰伦虫族 | Extra-galactic hive-swarm organism |
| T'au Empire | 钛帝国 | Young, technologically-advanced xenos civilization |
| Adeptus Mechanicus | 机械教 | The Imperium's tech-priesthood; worships the Omnissiah |
| Ecclesiarchy | 国教 | The Imperial Church; worships the Emperor as a god |
| Inquisition | 审判庭 | Imperial secret police and heresy-hunting organization |
| Astra Militarum | 星界军 | The Imperial Guard — the Imperium's regular military |
| Adeptus Astartes | 阿斯塔特修会 | The Space Marines — approximately one thousand Chapters |
| Adeptus Custodes | 禁军 | The Emperor's personal bodyguard; far beyond standard Space Marines |

### Core Concepts

| English | 中文 | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| The Warp | 亚空间 | A parallel dimension shaped by the emotions of all sentient life; serves as both FTL travel medium and the domain of Chaos |
| Astronomican | 星炬 | The Emperor's psychic beacon; the only navigational reference for Imperial fleets in the Warp |
| Golden Throne | 黄金王座 | The life-support device sustaining the Emperor's ruined body and powering the Astronomican |
| Primarch | 基因原体 | One of twenty superhuman demigods created by the Emperor; each commanded a Space Marine Legion |
| Space Marine | 星际战士 | Genetically-engineered super-soldier |
| Psyker | 灵能者 | An individual capable of channelling Warp energy |
| Warmaster | 战帅 | Supreme military commander during the Great Crusade |
| Exterminatus | 灭绝令 | The order to destroy an entire planet by extreme means |
| Era Indomitus | 不屈纪元 | The new era following Guilliman's resurrection (from ~M42) |

### The Chaos Gods

| English | 中文 | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Khorne | 恐虐 | The Blood God — lord of war and slaughter; seated upon the Brass Throne |
| Tzeentch | 奸奇 | The Changer of Ways — god of schemes, magic, and fate |
| Nurgle | 纳垢 | The Plague Lord — god of decay, stagnation, and despair; called "Grandfather" by his followers |
| Slaanesh | 色孽 | The Dark Prince — god of excess, indulgence, and perfection |

### Major Events

| English | 中文 | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Dark Age of Technology | 黑暗科技时代 | Humanity's golden age of technological achievement (M15–M25) |
| Age of Strife | 纷争时代 | The collapse of human civilization; interstellar travel severed by Warp storms (M25–M30) |
| Great Crusade | 大远征 | The Emperor's galaxy-spanning military campaign to reunite humanity (M30) |
| Horus Heresy | 荷鲁斯之乱 | The civil war triggered by Warmaster Horus's betrayal (M31) |
